	{A ByLaw providing for the rules of general governance and transaction of the affairs of the Corporation known as the Carleton Computer Science Society.} \\[0.1in]
	{\em Be in enacted by the Board of Directors and Members of the Corporation known as Carleton Computer Science Society as follows:}

\begin{enumerate}
	\item\marginpar{\em Short Title}This ByLaw may be henceforth cited as the {\em Consitution of the Carleton Computer Science Society}.

	\item\marginpar{\em Name of the Society}The Society shall be henceforth known as the {\em Carleton Computer Science Society}, and may be referred to as simply {\em the Society}.

	\item\marginpar{\em Definition of the Society Year}The {\em Society Year} shall be defined as the period between {\em May 1st} of the current {\em Calendar Year} and {\em April 31st} of the subsequent {\em Calendar Year}.

	\item\marginpar{\em Objects of the Society}The {\em Objects of the Society} shall be
		\begin{enumerate}
			\item to promote the social and academic values associated with the study of Computer Science at Carleton University;
			\item to provide services related to, but not limited to, socialization, academics and industry associated with the field of Computer Science; and,
			\item to maintain status as
				\begin{enumerate}
					 \item a departmental society of Carleton University, as per the {\em Accreditation of Student Organizations Policy}; and,		
					\item an incorporated not-for-profit organization, as per the pertainent Ontario Corporations legislation and regulations.
				\end{enumerate}
		\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}
